AAC 22MS LOW-DELAY PROFILE

Base: SBC30 KEEP30 SLOTS6 TIMER5 FRAMES4

- BT_KEEPALIVE_SILENCE_IDLE_MS = 30
- AUDIO_SLOT_COUNT_SBC = 6
- audio_timer_interval real do SBC = 5
- SBC_LOW_DELAY_FRAMES_PER_PACKET = 4

AAC-LC changed:
- AUDIO_SLOT_COUNT_AAC: 16 -> 1
- aac_audio_timer_interval: 12 -> 22 ms
- AAC-LC sends 1 frame per RTP packet
- samples_ready cap for AAC-LC = 1 frame

Math:
- 1 AAC-LC frame = 1024 samples
- 1024 / 48000 = 21.3 ms
- 22 ms timer gives about 0.7 ms margin
